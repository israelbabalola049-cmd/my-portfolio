Israel Babalola

Developer.
Problem solver.
Creative.

I am a frontend developer based in Lagos, Nigeria, with a disciplined eye for visual detail and a genuine passion for building things that work beautifully. I care deeply about both the aesthetics and the architecture of what I build, because great software should feel as good as it performs.

My background spans the full spectrum of the frontend, from structuring semantic HTML and responsive CSS to integrating REST APIs and managing complex application state. I have built everything from sleek marketing websites to multi-portal management systems, and I approach every project with the same curiosity and commitment.

When I am not writing code, I am studying design systems, exploring new interaction patterns, or finding better ways to bridge the gap between what designers imagine and what engineers build.
